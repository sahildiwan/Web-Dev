How do I join the club?

First, send us an e-mail introducing yourself. If you have an on-line portfolio make sure to include a link in order to show off your work. Secondly, just show up to a club meeting! Upcoming and past meeting information, including minutes and links to tutorials, are as shown below:

Schedule

January 9th | 6pm | Deschutes Room 100

Meeting Agenda: We will cover beginner to advanced details of HTML5 and CSS3. This meeting will allow us to get everyone caught up and comfortable creating a static website before we move on to creating dynamic websites. Beginner and intermediate students will go though a step-by-step tutorial of creating a full static HTML5 website. Our more advanced students will be learning the ins and outs of Bootstrap.
