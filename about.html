About the club

The “UO Web Dev Club” is a student run tutorial style club dedicated to teaching interested students of the University of Oregon the fundamental and advanced features of modern day web development. Each member on our board has web development experience. Prior experience with web development is not required, however, a basic understanding of front-end development (HTML, CSS, and JavaScript) is a plus. Ultimately, we intend and hope to make each of our members a full stack developer.
